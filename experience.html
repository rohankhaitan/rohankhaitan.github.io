    Some of the projects from different categories are in the following -

  • Image Classification Using Transfer Learning (Github Link)

    – Idea was to use a pre-trained model and Fine Tune it to achieve a good accuracy for predicting 15 scene categories. Fine Tuning is a very crucial and some time a very difficult step in Transfer Learning. InceptionResNetV2 model after Fine Tuning worked extremely well.

  • Building a RNN from Scratch for Sentiment Analysis (Github Link)

    – Built a Recurrent Neural Network from scratch to identify Sentiment. The idea was to understand how the network works, Backpropagation Through Time(BPTT) and carry out a sentiment anlysis without using built-in libraries like Keras.

  • Perturbed Gradient Descent to avoid Saddle points (Report)

    – The goal of this Theoretical Project was to understand how PGD and Projected PGD help to escape saddle points in case of unconstrained and constrained Optimization.

  • Dimensionality Reduction using Principal Component Analysis (Project Work)

    – Some benefits of dimensionality reduction include less computation/training time, removing multicollinearity by eliminating redundant features and also in visualization of multidimensional Data. The aim was to understand the mathematics behind PCA and also to see the applications in Machine Learning.

  • Gibberish Text Classification (Github Link)

    – Understanding the context based on words is not a good idea in this case. Here the aim was to focus on the k-grams or the characters. Basic ML models were tried out using the k-grams but CNN model based on characters was the best choice.

  • Predicting Famine in Countries

    – Forecasted the production, population and GDP for year 2025 from existing data for different countries (Tried ARIMA & LSTM model). Then Created an index based on these three and used it to predict whether a country will experience Famine or not (Classification - Used XgBoost Model). Also tried some ways to suggest preventive measures.

  • High Frequency Time Series Analysis (Report & Presentation)

    – Worked on hourly Energy Consumption Data. Purpose was to understand different models and find out the best one. Among Seasonal ARIMA, Fbprophet and LSTM models, LSTM worked better.

  • Real life implementation of Logistic Regression and dealing with Multi-collinearity (Report & Presentation)
      (Undergraduate Dissertation)

    – The aim was to see how logistic regression helps in classification and implement it. Multicollinearity is an important factor which effects the result significantly. The challenge was to detect it and adapt techniques to get rid of it.
